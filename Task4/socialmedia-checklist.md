# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use Checklist
.

## ✅ Do’s

-  **Think before you post**  
  Ensure your content is respectful, appropriate, and reflects your values.

-  **Protect your privacy**  
  Use strong passwords and regularly check your account privacy settings.

-  **Verify information before sharing**  
  Cross-check facts to avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.

-  **Maintain a professional online presence**  
  Remember that recruiters and institutions may view your profile.

-  **Respect others online**  
  Communicate politely and avoid unnecessary arguments or offensive comments.

---

## Don’ts

-  **Don’t share personal or sensitive information**  
  Avoid posting your address, phone number, or financial details.

-  **Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content**  
  It can harm your reputation and future opportunities.

-  **Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling**  
  Always promote a positive and respectful online environment.

-  **Don’t click on suspicious links or messages**  
  These may lead to scams or phishing attacks.

-  **Don’t overshare your daily activities**  
  It can create privacy and security risks.
