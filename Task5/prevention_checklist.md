#  Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)


---

##  Prevention Tips

-  **Use strong and unique passwords**  
  Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across platforms.

- **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**  
  Add an extra layer of security for email, social media, and banking apps.

-  **Never share OTPs or passwords**  
  No bank, app, or official authority will ever ask for your OTP.

-  **Verify links before clicking**  
  Avoid clicking on unknown or shortened links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.

-  **Be cautious on public Wi-Fi**  
  Avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts on public networks.

-  **Check app permissions regularly**  
  Only allow necessary permissions to apps installed on your phone.

---

##  UPI & Financial Safety Tips

-  **Do not accept unknown UPI collect requests**  
  Scammers may trick you into approving payments instead of receiving money.

-  **Double-check receiver details before payment**  
  Always verify the UPI ID or phone number before sending money.

-  **Avoid screen-sharing during financial transactions**  
  Fraudsters may ask for screen access to steal sensitive information.
